Why “Bridge of Hope”?

Bridge of Hope connects surplus food with those who need it most. We turn excess into nourishment and waste into hope—one meal at a time.

Mission & Vision

A bridge between excess food and empty plates

Our Mission

Connecting surplus food with trusted NGOs to reduce waste and provide dignified meals.

Our Vision

A future where no good food is wasted and no one goes hungry.

Who we serve

Orphanages & Child Homes

Providing safe and timely meals for children through nearby donors.

Old Age Homes

Supporting senior citizens with consistent, nutritious food donations.

Homeless & Outreach

Delivering meals to shelters and street outreach programs across the city.
